Welcome to Finland,

Finland has a rich heritage shaped by ancient traditions, Nordic history, and a deep respect for nature and equality. It is known for beautiful places like Helsinki, Lapland, thousands of lakes, forests, and the Northern Lights. Finnish food and culture feature simple, hearty dishes like salmon and rye bread, along with saunas, festivals, and a calm, nature-loving lifestyle.
